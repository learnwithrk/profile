About Me

Dr. Rajakrishnan Manivel is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Commerce (CA) at PSG College of Arts & Science, Coimbatore — one of India's most distinguished autonomous institutions. With 11+ years of teaching experience, he brings a rigorous yet engaging approach to Commerce, Finance, and Marketing.

His research spans digital banking, consumer behaviour, taxation, AI in business decision-making, rural entrepreneurship, and sustainability. He has authored one book, contributed 11+ book chapters, and published 23+ journal articles. He has presented extensively at national and international conferences.

Dr. Rajakrishnan holds a Ph.D. in Commerce, along with M.Com (CA), M.Phil, MBA, PGDIB, PGDIPR, UGC-NET, and TNSET. His work appears in Scopus, Web of Science, and UGC-indexed journals, Springer book volumes, Eliva Press, and SSRN.
