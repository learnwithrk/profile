About Me

Dr. Rajakrishnan Manivel is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Commerce (CA) at PSG College of Arts & Science, Coimbatore — one of India's most distinguished autonomous institutions. With over eight years of teaching and research experience, he brings a rigorous yet engaging approach to Commerce, Finance, and Marketing.

His research spans digital banking, consumer behaviour, taxation, AI in business decision-making, rural entrepreneurship, and sustainability — areas at the intersection of contemporary economic challenges and scholarly inquiry. He has presented his work at numerous ICSSR-sponsored national and international conferences and serves as an active resource person and session chair.

Dr. Rajakrishnan is a prolific academic author with publications in Scopus, Web of Science, and UGC-indexed peer-reviewed journals, a chapter contributor to Springer books, and a working paper author on SSRN. His qualifications include M.Com (CA), M.Phil, MBA, PGDIB, UGC-NET, TNSET, and a PGDIPR in Intellectual Property Law from IGNOU.
